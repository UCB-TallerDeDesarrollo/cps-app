Welcome to the Unsolved Problems!

Once you have selected the Laggins Skills you can create an Unsoved Problem.

Now we were going to show you how you can do that.

Add an Unsolved Problem

Just three steps:
  1. Tap on the icon
  2. Fill the input.
  3. Tap on create button.

Edit an Unsolved Problem

Just four steps:
  1. Swipe to the left to see options.
  2. Tap on the icon
  3. Fill your changes.
  4. Tap on save button.

Delete an Unsolved Problem

Just three steps:
  1. Swipe to the left to see options.
  2. Tap on the icon
  3. Tap OK in order to confirm the action.

Start the Plan B

Just one step:
  1. Tap on one Unsolved Problem.
  2. Or Swipe to the left to see options.
  3. Tap on the icon
  4. And tap the First Step: Emphaty Step.

Any questions?

You can click the help icon.
